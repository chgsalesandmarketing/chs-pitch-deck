Integrated Content Marketing & IMC Proposal

Unique · Personalised · Inviting — A revised approach for Courtleigh Hospitality Group

1. Three-Tiered Content Ecosystem

Tier 1: Flagship Episodes

  • Monthly 60 s mini-docs
  • 1 hr shoot + polished edit + VO + sound design
  • Partner cameo & intern support
  • Hub content that radiates Unique · Personalised · Inviting

Tier 2: Weekly Pillars

  • Caption-Loop Reels: 4–6 s hook → 10–15 s B-roll → mini-article caption
  • Event Promos: 15 s teasers sourced from flagship B-roll

Tier 3: Micro, Reactive & On-The-Fly Initiatives

  • Daily Stories & UGC: staff spotlights, guest selfies, morning-lobby snaps
  • On-The-Fly Reels: chef demos, hidden-corner tours, surprise arrivals
  • Pop-Up Promotions: limited-time offers & flash discounts
  • Event Tie-Ins: festival partnerships
  • User-Generated Campaigns: guest-themed content calls & reposts

5. Sample Episode Script

0–05 s

Visuals: Pinned comment (“Show us Mingles!”) fades in over glowing bar lights.

VO: “@foodiejane wondered what electrifies Mingles after dark—let’s slip in and listen.”

Audio & Notes: SFX: gentle whoosh on comment; undercurrent of low DJ beat.

05–12 s

Visuals: Glide past velvet stools to the DJ booth; neon “LIVE!” flickers.

VO: “Pull up a stool by DJ Mayhem—where the city’s night owls remix their rhythms.”

Audio & Notes: Music: light piano chords join DJ beat; bar ambience.

12–25 s

Visuals: Macro: ice clink in coupe; bartender sweeps rosemary sprig onto rim.

VO: “Hear that ice-clink? It’s our start gun—every note bends to your mood.”

Audio & Notes: SFX: crystal-clear ice-clink; soft cymbal wash.

25–40 s

Visuals: Slow-mo pour of Mingles Sangria; guests nod along.

VO: “This is Mingles Sangria—Hardy’s Pinot Grigio, coconut rum, pineapple & orange juices, a tropical twist that keeps you guessing.”

Audio & Notes: Grade: warm ambers; music rises under VO.

40–55 s

Visuals: Wide shot: crowd moving; DJ’s hand on turntable; neon “LIVE!”.

VO: “Insiders know: Mingles is where every Friday story finds its pulse.”

Audio & Notes: Music: DJ Mayhem drop; cut to bar chatter loop.

55–60 s

Visuals: Pull back to reveal full bar; overlay to pin next comment.

VO: “Where shall we wander next? Tell us below—or join us this Friday.”

Audio & Notes: SFX: comment ping; music tag loops.

Efficiency

One event shoot doubles as flagship + promo—max output, minimal days.

Consistency + Variety

Tier 1 delivers “wow,” Tier 2 sustains, Tier 3 reacts.

True IMC Power

Unified inviting, personalised story—no silos.
